This is Home of Technobytes Industries.

Technobytes Industries started as J.J.L.L. Labs, a cooperation formed by two friends. Eventually they broke up and split the cooperation. Jonathan B. Koo-Lau's side became J.J.Jamo Labs. The name would later be changed to Megabytes, and more recently Technobytes Industries.
Even now, four years later, Technobytes still isn't a real company. (FYI the friends were in second grade lol)
